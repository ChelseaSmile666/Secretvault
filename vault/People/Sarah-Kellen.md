# Sarah Kellen (now Sarah Kensington)

> **Named in public court documents.** Identified in grand jury records and civil filings as a recruiter and household manager for Epstein. Received immunity under the 2008 NPA (SDFL 08-80736). Now publicly known as Sarah Kensington. No criminal charges have been filed. Changed surname after Epstein's 2008 conviction.

## Role in Network (Per Court Record)

Per grand jury documents (released), civil filings, and court testimony in US v. Maxwell:

- Named in Palm Beach Police Department investigation (2005–2006) as person who scheduled Epstein's "massage" appointments
- Named in federal grand jury record as a recruiter for Epstein
- Palm Beach PD reportedly recommended charges against Kellen; SDFL declined under NPA
- Received immunity from federal prosecution as an "identified potential co-conspirator" under the 2008 NPA
- Appeared as subject of SDNY interest in 2019; ultimately not charged

---

## 2008 NPA Immunity (Public Record)

The NPA signed by Acosta (SDFL 08-80736) granted immunity to named individuals, including Kellen, who were identified in the federal investigation. This was one of the most-criticised aspects of the NPA revealed in the 2019 *Doe v. US* proceedings and in reporting.

## Post-2008 (Public Record)

- Changed surname to Kensington around 2010 per public reporting
- Married NASCAR driver Brian Vickers (public record; marriage announcement)
- Filed declaration in civil proceedings; invoked Fifth Amendment in depositions per civil case records
