## Tools
| Tool | URL | Purpose |
|------|-----|---------|
| Google Maps | https://maps.google.com | Plot + Street View |
| Satellites.pro | https://satellites.pro | Historical satellite |

## Format Converters

- DMS: `40°42'46"N 74°00'22"W`
