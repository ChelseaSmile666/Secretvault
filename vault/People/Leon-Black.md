# Leon Black

> **Paid Epstein $158 million between 2012–2017.** Co-founder and former CEO of Apollo Global Management. Stepped down as Apollo CEO in March 2021 following an independent review (Dechert LLP). Review found payments were for legitimate tax/estate services and found no evidence of involvement in criminal activity. Senate Finance Committee investigation ongoing re: whether payments funded Epstein's trafficking operations. **No criminal charges filed.**

## Payments to Epstein (Public Record)

| Period | Amount | Purpose (per Dechert review) |
|--------|--------|------------------------------|
| 2012–2017 | $158 million total | Tax and estate planning advice |

### Context
- Payments were made after Epstein's 2008 Florida conviction
- Black used Epstein for "tax, estate planning and other related services" per Dechert review
- Epstein's advice was described as vetted by other professional advisors
- Senate Finance Committee later investigated whether these payments financed Epstein's trafficking operations
