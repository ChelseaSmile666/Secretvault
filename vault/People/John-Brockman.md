## Edge Foundation — Epstein's Science Access Mechanism

### What is Edge?
- Edge Foundation hosts annual "question" events bringing together elite scientists, technologists, and intellectuals
- Annual dinner at luxury venue, invitation-only, attendees include Nobel laureates, tech billionaires, leading academics
- Brockman coined "Third Culture" — idea that scientists are the new public intellectuals
- Alumni/regulars: Richard Dawkins, Steven Pinker, Marvin Minsky, Stephen Hawking, Daniel Dennett, George Church, Craig Venter

### Epstein's Role at Edge
- Epstein became a major financial backer and de facto member of Edge circle
- Epstein attended Edge dinners and was introduced to leading scientists through Brockman's network
- Brockman facilitated connections between Epstein and scientists who later received Epstein grants
- Epstein used Edge network to recruit academics into his "science philanthropy" operation
- See [[Findings/Harvard-MIT-Academic-Network]]

### Post-2008 Continued Access
- Brockman continued including Epstein in Edge events after 2008 conviction
- Edge dinners took place at Epstein properties including NYC townhouse
- Multiple scientists later expressed regret about contact maintained through Brockman's network
