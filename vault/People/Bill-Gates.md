## Connection to Epstein — Documented

### Admitted Meetings (Post-Conviction)
Gates admitted meeting Epstein **after** Epstein's 2008 Florida conviction, when his sex offender status was public knowledge:
- Met Epstein in New York starting ~2011
- Called the relationship a "huge mistake" in 2021 (DealBook interview, NYT)
- Said he was "naive" about Epstein's motives

### Nature of Meetings (Reported)
- Epstein pitched Gates on a global health fund — which Gates declined
- Meetings at Epstein's [[Locations/9-East-71st-Street-NYC]] townhouse
- NYT reported (2019) that Gates met Epstein multiple times between 2011–2014
- JPMorgan executive Jes Staley (see [[People/Jes-Staley]]) facilitated early introductions

### Gates Foundation / JPMorgan Connection
- Epstein claimed — and documents partially support — he was advising the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation on a $2B health charity fund #claim
- Internal Gates Foundation emails showed staff growing uncomfortable with Epstein relationship
- Mary Barra and other corporate figures reportedly met Epstein at Gates-adjacent events #claim

### Divorce Connection
- Melinda Gates cited Bill's relationship with Epstein as one of her reasons for discomfort and ultimately divorce (reported by WSJ, 2021) #claim
- Melinda met Epstein once, found him "abhorrent" (her words, DealBook 2022)
