## Connection to Epstein

- Epstein donated **$6.5 million** to Harvard — the largest single gift to the math department at the time
- Epstein also donated to the Program for Evolutionary Dynamics at Harvard
- Donations facilitated access to Harvard faculty, labs, and resources
- Summers was Harvard President during peak of Epstein donations (2001–2006)
- Harvard initially refused to return Epstein donations post-2008; controversy reignited after 2019 arrest

### Social Connection
- Summers and Epstein overlapped in elite New York and academic circles

- No direct documented meeting has been reported, but institutional connection through Harvard is documented

### Harvard's Epstein Problem
- Harvard received $9.1 million total from Epstein (Harvard Crimson investigation)
- Post-2019 Harvard announced it would not return funds already spent
