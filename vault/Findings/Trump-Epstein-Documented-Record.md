## Social Circle Overlap — Documented (Public Record)

- Trump and Epstein both operated in New York/Palm Beach high-society circles from the late 1980s through early 2000s
- Trump is documented in flight logs as having traveled on Epstein's aircraft
- Both were members of Mar-a-Lago (Trump) and the social scene around it
- Trump gave an interview to New York magazine in 2002 describing Epstein as a "terrific guy" who "likes beautiful women as much as I do"
- Trump stated they had a falling out around 2004 and had not spoken in the years before Epstein's 2019 death

## 1994 Mar-a-Lago Court Document (Public Record)

Per a court document in the Epstein case files:
- A 14-year-old girl described being taken to Mar-a-Lago in 1994
- She stated Epstein introduced her to the property's owner, Donald Trump
- The document quotes Epstein allegedly elbowing Trump and asking: "This is a good one, right?" with Trump reportedly smiling and nodding
- **This is a statement in a court filing from a victim, not a proven fact in court proceedings. It is documented here as a civil/investigative claim (#claim).**
