## "Perversion of Justice" (2018)

- Three-part investigation published November 28, 2018
- Detailed how Acosta's 2008 NPA violated federal law by not notifying victims (Crime Victims' Rights Act)
- Interviewed 60+ Epstein victims
- Documented the full scope of the trafficking operation for the first time publicly
- Directly triggered DOJ review of the NPA and Epstein's subsequent arrest (July 2019)
- Won multiple journalism awards; basis for Netflix documentary

### What Made It Break Through
- Brown worked for a regional paper — less susceptible to elite advertiser pressure
- Spent 18+ months building victim testimony with corroboration
- FOIA'd court records that established the NPA violation of victims' rights
