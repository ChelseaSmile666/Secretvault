## Connection to Epstein

### Legal Representation
- Dershowitz was part of the legal team that negotiated the 2008 Florida Non-Prosecution Agreement (NPA) on behalf of Epstein
- Co-led defence alongside [[People/Alexander-Acosta]] counterpart negotiations
- The NPA is documented in [[Findings/2008-NPA-Florida]] and [[Findings/DOJ-NPA-Decision-Makers]]
- Dershowitz has publicly defended the NPA as appropriate given evidence at the time

### Civil Allegations (#claim)
- Virginia Giuffre alleged in civil filings that she was directed to have sex with Dershowitz on multiple occasions #claim
- Dershowitz categorically and consistently denies all allegations
- Filed defamation suits against accusers and their attorneys
- In 2022 Giuffre withdrew allegation against Dershowitz; Dershowitz characterised this as vindication
- Giuffre v. Dershowitz case settled (terms not disclosed)

### Harvard Connection
- Dershowitz was close with Epstein through Harvard circles
- Epstein donated to Harvard Law School programs
- Dershowitz reportedly socialized at Epstein properties — acknowledged visiting Palm Beach home
- See [[Findings/Harvard-MIT-Academic-Network]]

## NPA Role (Documented)

Per [[Findings/2008-NPA-Florida]] and DOJ Inspector General review:
- Dershowitz helped negotiate the NPA that granted Epstein immunity from federal prosecution
- The agreement also granted immunity to "potential co-conspirators" — a provision later found highly irregular by DOJ IG
- Dershowitz disputes characterisation of the deal as improper
