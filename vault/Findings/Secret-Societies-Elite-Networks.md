## Bohemian Grove

### What It Is
- Annual two-week gathering, July, Monte Rio, California
- Founded 1872; membership by invitation only; estimated 2,000+ members
- Attendees: US presidents, corporate CEOs, military, media, academics
- Famous "Cremation of Care" ceremony — theatrical ritual burning effigy

### Leaked Membership / Documentation
- Alex Jones infiltrated and filmed Cremation of Care (2000) — footage widely distributed
