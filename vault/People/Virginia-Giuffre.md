# Virginia Giuffre (née Roberts)

> **Deceased — April 25, 2025.** Primary named accuser in the Epstein network. Died by suicide at age 41. Her posthumous memoir *Nobody's Girl* was published October 21, 2025. Filed civil lawsuit Giuffre v. Maxwell (SDNY 15-CV-07433) in 2015, voluntarily waiving anonymity. Documents released by Judge Preska's order (Jan 2024).

## Posthumous Memoir — *Nobody's Girl* (2025)

**Nobody's Girl: A Memoir of Surviving Abuse and Fighting for Justice**
- Co-written with Amy Wallace
- Published posthumously: October 21, 2025
- Publisher: Alfred A. Knopf (Penguin Random House)

### Key Documented Contents (Per Published Reviews / Journalism)

- Begins with childhood abuse she says started around age 7; names her father as her "original betrayer"
- Describes her time working for Trump at Mar-a-Lago (her father also worked there); describes being groomed by Maxwell and Epstein
- Describes sadomasochistic sexual abuse by Epstein
- Alleges three occasions of rape by Prince Andrew — in London, New York, and on Little Saint James island
- States Maxwell woke her one day in March 2001 telling her she would meet a "handsome prince"; describes Prince Andrew correctly guessing she was 17
- Giuffre was writing the book; in the weeks before her April 2025 death, she made public accusations that her husband had physically abused her during their 22-year marriage and wanted to revise the book — published version contains her original descriptions of him alongside editorial notes acknowledging the later allegations
