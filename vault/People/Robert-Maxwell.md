# Robert Maxwell

> **Status: Deceased** — Found dead November 5, 1991, floating near his yacht Lady Ghislaine off the Canary Islands. British media magnate; father of [[People/Ghislaine-Maxwell]]. Documented connections to intelligence services. Death ruled accidental drowning; multiple investigators consider it suspicious.

## Intelligence Connections — Documented and Alleged

### Mossad (Documented in Journalism and Memoirs)
- Ari Ben-Menashe (former Israeli intelligence officer) identified Maxwell as a Mossad asset in his memoir *Profits of War* (1992)
- Gordon Thomas and Martin Dillon documented Mossad connection in *Robert Maxwell: Israel's Superspy* (2002)
- Seymour Hersh referenced Maxwell's intelligence role in *The Samson Option* (1991)
- Israeli state funeral: PM Yitzhak Shamir, PM Shimon Peres, six serving and former heads of Israeli intelligence attended — extraordinary for a foreign businessman
- Former Mossad director Nahum Admoni reportedly present at funeral

### MI6 / British Intelligence
- British parliamentary MI5/MI6 files on Maxwell remain classified
- Multiple biographers note Maxwell's unusual access to intelligence circles across Cold War
- KGB: Some accounts allege Maxwell also passed information to Soviet intelligence #claim

### PROMIS Software Alleged Role #claim
- Ben-Menashe and others allege Maxwell was involved in distribution of bugged PROMIS database software to foreign governments — enabling intelligence collection #claim
- Inslaw Inc. case (US congressional investigation) touched on PROMIS; Maxwell's role alleged but not proven #claim
