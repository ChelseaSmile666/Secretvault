## Connection to Epstein

### The Facilitator
- Negroponte was a founding figure at MIT Media Lab and maintained significant social influence there
- He facilitated or endorsed the introduction of Epstein to the MIT Media Lab network
- When Joi Ito was seeking donors, Negroponte was one who encouraged the Epstein relationship despite the 2008 conviction
- MIT's independent review (Goodwin Procter, 2019) identified Negroponte as a figure in the Epstein introduction

### Own Statement
- Negroponte acknowledged facilitating the Epstein-MIT connection
- Expressed regret but noted he believed Epstein's philanthropy was genuine
- Was not charged with any wrongdoing
