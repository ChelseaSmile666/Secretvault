# Masha Bucher (née Drokova)

> **Named ~1,600 times in DOJ Epstein files.** Russian-born tech VC investor and former PR consultant. Worked as Epstein's PR agent — advising him on media strategy to rehabilitate his public image after his 2008 conviction. Now a Silicon Valley venture capitalist (Day One Ventures). Appeared in unreleased 2017 Shawn Ryan Show interview, which began circulating in February 2026. Not charged with any wrongdoing.
