# 2024 Document Release — Giuffre v. Maxwell

> **Verified public record.** Judge Loretta Preska (SDNY) ordered the release of previously sealed documents from the civil lawsuit Virginia Giuffre v. Ghislaine Maxwell. Documents released in batches beginning January 3, 2024. Available via PACER.

---

## Background

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Case | Giuffre v. Maxwell, SDNY 15-CV-07433 |
| Filed | September 21, 2015 by Virginia Giuffre (née Roberts) |
| Claim | Defamation (Maxwell called Giuffre a liar re: abuse allegations) |
| Settlement | ~2017; amount undisclosed |
| Documents | Sealed at time of settlement |
| Release order | Judge Loretta Preska, December 2023 |
| Docs released | Batches from January 3, 2024 onward |

---

## What the Documents Contain (Per Court Record)

The released documents include:
- Depositions of Epstein, Maxwell, and others
- Correspondence between parties
- Lists of names contained in discovery materials
- Flight logs and travel records submitted as evidence
- Internal communications from Epstein household

The "Lolita Express" flight logs (Epstein's Boeing 727) were submitted as evidence and show travel between:
- Teterboro, NJ (TEB)
- Palm Beach, FL (PBI)
- St. Thomas, USVI (STT) → [[Locations/Little-Saint-James-USVI]]
- Santa Fe, NM (SAF) → [[Locations/Zorro-Ranch-New-Mexico]]

Logs show names of passengers alongside Epstein and Maxwell on multiple flights. Log interpretation is contested in some cases.
