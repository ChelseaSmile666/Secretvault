## Connection to Epstein

### Edge Foundation
- Pinker is a central figure of [[People/John-Brockman]]'s Edge Foundation
- Regularly attended Edge events including those at Epstein venues
- Part of the intellectual network Epstein cultivated

### 2007 Character Reference — Documented
- During pre-trial negotiations that led to the 2008 NPA, Epstein's legal team solicited character references from prominent scientists
- Pinker provided a letter that was used in Epstein's defence negotiations
- Pinker has said he did not realise the letter would be used as a character reference in criminal proceedings — he believed he was providing professional context about Epstein's science interests
- This is documented in reporting on the NPA process

### Post-2019 Statement
- Pinker acknowledged the connection and expressed regret about the character reference usage
- Maintained he was unaware of the full scope of Epstein's crimes when in contact
