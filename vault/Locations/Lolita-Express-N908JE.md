## The Massage Table

- The 727 was fitted with a specially configured interior including a **massage table/bed**
- Multiple flight attendants and victims described this feature in testimony
- The aircraft configuration was designed to facilitate the same "massage" activities as Epstein's properties
- Flight attendants testified about being pressured or witnessing inappropriate activity on board

## Second Aircraft — Gulfstream

- Epstein also operated a Gulfstream jet (smaller, private, N121JE and others)
