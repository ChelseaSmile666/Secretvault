## Epstein Files Action

### Floor Speech — 2026-02-10
- Visited DOJ alongside Rep. [[People/Thomas-Massie]] (R-KY) and spent approximately two hours reviewing unredacted Epstein documents
- During a House floor speech, publicly named six men whose identities had been redacted in the released files
- DOJ officials acknowledged their redaction mistakes when the errors were identified
- Stated: *"If we found six men that they were hiding in two hours, imagine how many men they are covering up for in those 3m files"*
- Noted the majority of files remain redacted
- Did not provide evidence of wrongdoing against any of the six men, and none have been charged with a crime in connection with Epstein

### Six Names Revealed
1. [[People/Les-Wexner]] — retail billionaire; FBI appeared to label co-conspirator
2. [[People/Sultan-Ahmed-bin-Sulayem]] — CEO of DP World
3. Nicola Caputo — reportedly part of photo lineup with no documented Epstein connection #claim
4. Salvatore Nuara — reportedly part of photo lineup with no documented Epstein connection #claim
5. Zurab Mikeladze — reportedly part of photo lineup with no documented Epstein connection #claim
6. Leonic Leonov — reportedly part of photo lineup with no documented Epstein connection #claim

> Per Guardian reporting: four of the six men had no connection to Epstein and were simply part of a law enforcement photo lineup. Two men who spoke to the Guardian denied knowing Epstein and said they had prior NYPD arrests for unrelated crimes, which likely explains how their photos ended up in the array.
