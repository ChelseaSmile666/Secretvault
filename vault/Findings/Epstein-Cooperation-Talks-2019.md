# Epstein Cooperation Talks — Documented FBI Record

> **Verified from released DOJ files.** Two separate documents in the January 30, 2026 release confirm that Epstein's defense attorneys met with SDNY prosecutors and FBI agents on 2019-07-29 — approximately 11 days before Epstein's death on 2019-08-10 — to discuss the possibility of cooperation and case resolution.

---

## Key Documents

| Document Title | Details |
|---|---|
| "Epstein Investigation Summary & Timeline" | FBI-attributed document; confirms 2019-07-29 meeting between FBI, SDNY prosecutors, and Epstein defense attorneys |
| "Jeffrey Epstein Significant Case Notification" | Closely resembles FBI memo; not attributed to specific agency; provides additional detail on meeting content |

---

## Meeting Content (Per Documents)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Date | 2019-07-29 |
| Parties | FBI, SDNY prosecutors, Epstein defense attorneys |
| Subject | Possibility of case resolution; possibility of Epstein cooperation |
| Specificity | Defense counsel discussed possibility "in very general terms" — no specific proposal made |
| Nature of cooperation | Documents state defense counsel "did not indicate what the nature of Epstein's cooperation might be, if any" |
| SDNY instruction | Defense counsel told to contact SDNY "if Epstein was prepared to accept responsibility for his conduct and/or they had a specific proposal" |

---

## Significance

- Meeting occurred approximately 11 days before Epstein's death on 2019-08-10
- No cooperation agreement was reached or formally proposed
- Documents do not indicate whether potential targets of cooperation were identified
- Identity of who Epstein might have cooperated against is not disclosed in released documents
